Improve your Interview Skills:

Activity: Mock Interview

  • Ask a parent or friend to act as the potential employer as you practice the interview skills previously learned.
  • Recap and Review how to prepare for an interview.
    • Research a company, organization, or school to prepare yourself for the different types of interviews.
    • Review important things to know for an interview with a school.
    • Review important things to know for an interview with a potential employer.
    • Review important things to know for an interview with a volunteer program.
    • Review and update your resume.
    • Review common mistakes and how to prevent those mistakes.
    • Be aware of time management.
    • Use proper language, posture, and wear the appropriate apparel.
