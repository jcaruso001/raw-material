Senior Year: January

Briefly review the “What Next?” from December.

Putting It All Together

  • Now that it is January, this is the time to be sure you have completed the previous checklist; marking off everything that is needed to be turned into the colleges of your choice.
  • January is usually the last month (for many colleges) to apply for fall admission.
  • Make sure you fill out all necessary forms for any final applications or for colleges that have accepted you.
  • Submit your FAFSA form as soon as possible.


ACTIVITY: Narrow down your schools of acceptance to a top five if you have been accepted to more than five.

What Next? Double-check to be sure that you have heard back from all the colleges to which you applied. If not, call them.

Staying on Track...

  • Review your list of schools and application/admission progress.
  • Search and apply for scholarships that are due within the next month for seniors.
