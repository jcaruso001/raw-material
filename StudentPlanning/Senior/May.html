Senior Year: May

Briefly review the “What Next?” from April.

College Year-by-Year Success

  • Set up a meeting with your high school counselor to discuss how to prepare for college.
  • Set up a visit at your new college campus and meet with your department and/or academic advisor over the summer to discuss how to prepare.
  • Make a checklist for the first semester of college.
  • Discuss with your college advisor how to set up your class schedule to remain on track for college graduation.
  • Discuss opportunities with your college advisor about how to get involved on campus, as well as off campus.
  • Express to your college advisor your enthusiasm about being a new member of the school and explain that you want to take advantage of internship(s) and/or study away opportunities.


What Next? Review all deadlines for orientation, selection of courses, selection of housing and meal plans, and tuition payment. Set up an appointment with your department and/or college advisor over the summer before fall classes begin.

Staying on Track...

  • Search and apply for scholarships that are due within the next month for seniors.
  • Plan to visit your new college over the summer months to familiarize yourself with the campus and to ask questions.
  • Once school is out for the summer, update you high school portfolio for the final time.
  • Stay active this summer! Read; take classes and/or work, intern, or job shadow.

CONGRATULATIONS on graduating high school and best wishes in college!
