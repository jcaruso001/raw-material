Finding Information on Colleges

ACTIVITY: Create a comparison chart of different college environments and choose environments which best suit your preferences.

  1. Large | Small
  2. Public | Private
  3. In-State | Out-of-State
  4. Religiously Affiliated | Not

Consider each topic heading above and make a list of what appeals most to you. You may even include distance from home, liberal arts colleges, university style, size of the institution, community or junior college, specialty colleges, single-gender schools, or schools with a religious focus.

Review this website to learn more about the differences between liberal arts colleges and universities: https://www.collegechoice.net/liberal-arts-college-or-research-university-whats-the-difference/
