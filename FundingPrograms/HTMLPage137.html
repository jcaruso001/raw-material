Wrap Up

Military Service is a career and it is a profession. It is a calling. It is not for everyone. It is not for everyone because it is hard to do. If you understand it, have a desire for it, and the capability for it, I challenge you to step to the plate. It is great life and a great career. Please look at some of the information we have provided. Look at some of the websites that were outlined and consider the military as a possible career jump-start or a viable way for you to finance your college education.
