U.S. Armed Forces Active Duty

Active Duty Armed Forces – what they are – not the fastest route to college degrees due to immediate active duty requirements for 2 to 6 years. They do provide the opportunity to gain some real-world experience, time-management skills, savings, and fantastic college financing programs, which all significantly increase the student’s ability to attend and graduate from any college at the end of their tour of service. This is an option for the student not yet ready for the academic demands of college or those that want the work experience first.
