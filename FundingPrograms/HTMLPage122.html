U.S. Service Academies

U.S. Service Academies are breeding grounds for the future leaders, CEOs, and senior executives for both military service and corporate America. They provide some of the strongest education and academic programs in the world. The academies are looking for the nation’s top 1% of the high school graduating class. Demanding entry requirements and even more demanding well-rounded academic and athletic student background make these institutions some of the most prestigious in the world. Selected for entry into any of these institutions equals a highly valued, free education.
