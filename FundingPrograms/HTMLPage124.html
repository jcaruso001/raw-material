Reserve Officer Training Corps (ROTC)

ROTC participation and scholarships are offered by the United States Army, Navy, and Air Force. They significantly reduce or eliminate college financing costs through:

  • part-time pay
  • book and fee allowances
  • possible room and board waivers
  • college tutors
  • tuition payments

Many variations of ROTC scholarships exist for each service branch. You will want to search for your best option using the websites in the following Practical Exercise.
