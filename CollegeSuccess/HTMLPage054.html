I can do this! (a few helpful hints to succeed in college)

Academic Success:

  • Get and stay organized
  • Attend class
  • Pick the ‘worst’ seat
  • Set a study schedule
  • Ask for help
  • Finals
  • Early grades are important

Social Success:

  • Realize it might take time to find “your group”
  • Attend school activities
  • Join clubs freshman year
  • Make an impact

General Success:

  • Make sure you get enough sleep
  • Make healthy eating choices
  • Take time to exercise
  • Get a job on campus
  • Learn how to manage your money
  • Realize you are laying the foundation for your college career and beyond
