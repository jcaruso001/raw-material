What will I need?

Below is a checklist of items you should plan to take to college.

For your room:

  • Bed sheets (twin XL for most)
  • Mattress pad
  • Comforter
  • Blanket
  • Pillows (2 to prop up in bed)
  • Bath towels (wash cloths)
  • Storage crates/shelves
  • Clothes hangers
  • Over-the-door hooks
  • Desk lamp and bulbs
  • Decorations/family photos
  • Removable wall mounts (for hanging décor)
  • Area rug
  • Air freshener
  • Cleaning supplies (all-purpose spray cleaner, paper towels)
  • Small vacuum cleaner or Bissell broom
  • Kitchen towels
  • Coffee pot
  • Coffee mugs/cups
  • Utensils (forks, spoons, knives)
  • Plates and bowls (non-breakable)
  • Cups (non-breakable)
  • Microwave or hot plate (if allowed)
  • Microwave safe plate
  • Toaster or toaster oven (if needed)
  • Can opener
  • Extension cord
  • Iron/ironing board
  • Laundry bag/hamper
  • Laundry supplies (detergent, dryer sheets, stain stick)
  • Trash can and trash bags
  • Clothes pins/chip clips
  • Drying rack
  • Small fan
  • Supply of snacks

For you:

  • Cell phone/charging cords
  • Ear buds (extra pair is useful)
  • Seasonal clothing (winter coat to summer swimsuit)
  • Dressy outfits and shoes
  • Shower shoes
  • Shower caddie
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Toilet paper/Kleenex
  • First Aid kit (including a thermometer)
  • Medicines (cold, headache, etc.)
  • Water bottle (reusable)
  • Backpack
  • Good walking shoes
  • Rain gear (jacket/umbrella)
  • Bike, helmet, bike lock, reflective gear
  • Key chain or lanyard
  • Small sewing kit
  • Safety pins

For your studies:

  • Computer
  • Computer cords/chargers
  • Notebooks
  • Stapler/staples
  • Pens/pencils (pencil sharpener)
  • Highlighters
  • Paper clips
  • Rubber bands
  • Scissors
  • Sticky notes
  • Printer/ink/computer paper
  • Calculator
  • Organizer

Things to consider:

  • What will my roommate bring? Do we want to coordinate decorations?
  • Are mini refrigerators allowed? If so, which roommate will bring one?
  • Is there a community kitchen? If yes, do I want to take items to cook? What small appliances are available in the kitchen?
  • Can the bed height be adjusted, or should I take bed risers so I can store stuff underneath?
  • If my bed is raised, will I need a step stool? What will I use as a bedside table?
  • Should I take an extra chair for my room…like a folding chair?
  • Do I have a shared bathroom that my roommate(s) and I are responsible for cleaning? Will we need a bathmat?
  • Do I really need to take 30 t-shirts and 2 heavy coats? Storage space is limited.
  • Will I need more heavy or light weight clothing?
  • If I go somewhere for the weekend, what will I use to pack in?
