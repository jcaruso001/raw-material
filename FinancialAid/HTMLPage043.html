First, Understand the FAFSA and EFC:

Before you begin your financial aid search, you will need to have a firm grasp of exactly what the FAFSA and EFC mean to you and your college costs.

The FAFSA – Free Application for Federal Student Aid: The Department of Education uses the information provided on the FAFSA to determine eligibility for federal aid. 

https://blog.collegeboard.org/what-is-the-fafsa 

https://studentaid.gov/h/understand-aid

https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa 

The EFC – Expected Family Contribution: A calculation colleges use to determine how much aid you might receive if you attend their institution.
