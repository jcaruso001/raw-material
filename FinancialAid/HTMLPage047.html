Appealing Your Financial Aid Package

In some cases, the financial aid offered by a college is not quite enough. While not always successful, you can appeal the offer in hopes of more aid. Just make sure you:

  • Understand the college’s appeal process
  • Appeal before you attend
  • Identify recent job loss, illness, and/or significant changes to your (your family’s) income are all good appeal comments
  • Do not try bidding wars between colleges
