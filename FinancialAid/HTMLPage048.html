Where to Find Scholarships

It is not possible to give a complete list of all scholarships available to college students. The key is targeting the specific search locations that provide the easiest and most pertinent search options.

Personal Contacts:

Inquire as to scholarships available through parents’/guardian’s employer or through your own employment. Do you have a part-time job with a company that offers college aid?

College Scholarship/Financial Aid Office

Ask about scholarships that are specific to the colleges where you are applying.

Civic Organizations

Check out community foundations and clubs such as Lions, Kiwanis, Rotary, Elk, etc. Contact local chapters in your town to see what local or national programs have available.

Religious Organizations

Check with your local Pastor, Priest, Rabbi, or Cleric about local and national scholarship opportunities.

High School Counselor

Discuss scholarship options available.
