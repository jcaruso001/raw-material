What is College Financial Packaging

A college financing package includes any monetary aspect that will reduce a student’s overall college cost. By piecing together different sources of income or discounts, your out-of-pocket expenses could make college a very affordable venture. Possible sources include:

  • Federal Aid – grants, loans, work-study
  • Scholarships – link to scholarship unit
  • Military awards – Link to military unit
  • Privately sourced college loans - https://loans.usnews.com/student-loans
  • PLUS loans - https://studentaid.gov/understand-aid/types/loans/plus
  • College fee waivers - https://studentloanhero.com/featured/fee-waiver-college-application/
  • Creative options – link to creative options unit
  • Negotiated reduced tuition - https://thescholarshipsystem.com/blog-for-students-families/5-steps-to-negotiate-college-tuition-and-save-thousands-of-dollars/
  • Student discounts on computers, software, and travel (for that flight home)
