Subtest 8: Mechanical Comprehension (MC)

Time: 19 minutes for 25 questions

Special instructions: Scratch paper allowed. Calculator not allowed. The Mechanical Comprehension subtest evaluates your understanding of mechanical principles and devices, including questions on basic mechanics and physical principles.
