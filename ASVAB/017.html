Subtest 9: Assembling Objects (AO)

Time:15 minutes for 25 questions

Special instructions: Scratch paper allowed. Calculator not allowed. The Assembling Objects subtest measures your spatial reasoning skills, particularly your ability to understand and manipulate two-dimensional shapes and how they fit together.

We have included several sample questions for this section below; for more information and practice questions, please see the official ASVAB site for practice questions pertaining to the Assembling Objects (AO) section: https://www.officialasvab.com/assembling-objects-ao/
