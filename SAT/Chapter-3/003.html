Chapter Three: Graphics on the SAT

Several types of graphics will appear on both the Reading section and the Writing and Language section of the SAT. These graphics can include photographs, diagrams, tables, and various types of graphs. The questions regarding these graphics may ask you to examine the graphic itself and answer questions or use the graphic along with the text to answer questions. Just like interpreting a reading passage, you will need to “read” the graphics to understand them and how they relate to the passages with which they are paired. Several types of graphics are presented as examples of material you may see on the test. The list is not exhaustive.
