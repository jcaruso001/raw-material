Trapezoids

A trapezoid is a quadrilateral having one pair of parallel sides. Since AB || DC, x + y = 180° and w + z = 180°. The height is the vertical distance between DC and AB.

A trapezoid could be described as a triangle that had its pointy top removed. As a result, a trapezoid has a top and a bottom made of horizontal flat surfaces referred to as the bases.
