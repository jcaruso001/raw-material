Chapter Four: Frustrations with Fractions

If fractions get you frustrated, you are not alone. Many students are uncomfortable working with fractions. A fraction is used to describe a part of a whole. The numerator (top) describes the part, and the denominator (bottom) describes the whole. In the fraction 2/3, the numerator is 2 and the denominator is 3. We'll discuss operations such as addition and multiplication in a later section. For now, we just want you to familiarize yourself with the concepts of equivalent fractions, reducing fractions, and common denominators.
