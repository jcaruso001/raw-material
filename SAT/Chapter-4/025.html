Solving Algebraic Inequalities

Definitions

An inequality is a statement that one quantity is not strictly equal to another quantity. Inequalities are shown using four symbols:

• Greater than: >
4 > 1
1 > –3
2 > √2

• Greater than or equal to: ≥
8 ≥ 2
2 + 3 ≥ 5

• Less than: <
0 < 4
–6 < -5

• Less than or equal to: ≤
6 – 2 ≤ 5
5/2 ≤ 2.5
