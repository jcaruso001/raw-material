Parallelograms

A parallelogram is a four-sided figure in which both pairs of opposite sides are parallel, both pairs
of opposite angles are equal, and both pairs of opposite sides are equal in length.

The area of a parallelogram is its base times its height, but due to its shape, the height of a parallelogram is not always equal to one of its sides. To find the height of a parallelogram, you must draw a perpendicular line from the base to the top of the figure.

Area of a parallelogram = base x height
