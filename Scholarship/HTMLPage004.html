Websites to Research

http://www.collegescholarships.com
Lists both free and fee-based scholarship sites and contains valuable financial aid and loan sources

www.feea.org/programs/scholarships
A site designated to assist children of federal employees (including military) identify funding options

https://fisherhouse.org/programs/
This site covers scholarship search engines, government programs, and general financial aid terms and information for military families

www.scholarships.com
Site similar to FastWeb.com. Search over 3.7 million college scholarships and grants

www.unigo.com
Free scholarship search divided into search categories such as merit-based, company-sponsored, military, minority, etc.

www.niche.com
Similar to Fastweb and Scholarships.com. Matches your interests with available scholarships.

www.petersons.com
A college and scholarship search engine

www.careeronestop.org
Department of Labor website to help undergraduates and graduates with scholarship searches, career exploration, training, and jobs. Click on the “Find Training” tab.

www.sallemae.com/scholarshipsearch
Free access to millions of undergraduate and graduate scholarships. Click on the “College Planning” tab.

www.ScholarshipAmerica.org
The nation’s largest nonprofit, private scholarship organization.

www.fastweb.com
This is the most well-known college scholarship search engine.
