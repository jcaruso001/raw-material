The Big Day

Things to remember for your interview:

  • Meet at the college or in a public place 
  • Arriving 10 minutes early is considered on time 
  • Dress appropriately
  • Address the interviewer by name or title 
  • Use good manners
  • Use a firm handshake, good posture, and maintain eye contact
  • Use your resume
  • Thank them for their time and send a follow up email
