Army/Navy/Marine Corps College Funds

The Army, Navy, and Marine Corps each provide tuition assistance (a "kicker") in addition to what you earn through the GI Bill. Each service controls the amount of extra money it provides. The Army fund increases the amount of support to as much as $70,000; the Navy fund increases the amount of support to as much as $50,000, and the Marine Corps fund increases the amount of support also to as much as $50,000. College Funds are awarded on a competitive basis according to academic merit (i.e., scoring in the top half of the Armed Services Vocational Aptitude Battery). To qualify one must also agree to serve six years.
