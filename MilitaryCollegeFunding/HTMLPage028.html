Montgomery GI Bill

Under the various GI Bills, the Armed Services (Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps and Coast Guard and their Reserves and National Guard) are able to help their members and eligible veterans cover the costs associated with getting an education or training. GI Bills provide up to 36 months of education benefits to eligible veterans for college, business, technical or vocational courses, correspondence courses, apprenticeship/job training and flight training. GI Bill benefits may be used while on active duty or after a fully honorable discharge from active duty.

GI Bill benefit amounts are adjusted each year according to changes in the Consumer Price Index (CPI). To qualify for the GI Bill, recruits must agree to have $100 deducted from their pay each month.
