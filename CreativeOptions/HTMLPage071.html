Part-time Job(s)

Consider part-time work during your college years. Search for jobs associated with your academic major. For example, if you are majoring in nursing, a job in a hospital or medical office would be a great fit and give you lots of experience in your field. Make sure to keep your priorities in check and only work the number of hours that allow you to maintain good grades and complete the required coursework to graduate.  

After college, the job market is very competitive, so you will need to build your work resume during college. Working a part-time job job demonstrates reliability, time management skills, responsibility, and an aptitude for the discipline in which you are studying. 

Caution: You will need to weigh the pros and cons of off-campus vs on campus-jobs as well as the number of hours you work.
