Shorten your Time in College

The average college graduate takes 5-1/2 years to get a 4-year degree. Decide now to earn your college degree in 4 years. Shortening your time in college will save you a tremendous amount of money. One year of college (tuition, room, board, travel, etc.) could cost $20,000- $40,000 easily, so graduating in 4 years can save you a lot in the long run.

How do you shorten your time in college?  

Consider taking classes instead of taking a break during your off semesters (Winter, Summer breaks).  

If you are enrolled in a 4-year college, check out schools near you such as a community college for more classes during the summer or on weekends when students would not normally be in class. Taking online classes during this time is also a great option.
