Plastic Perils - Credit Cards

College students need to:  

  • Gain a credit history  
  • Have access to additional money (mostly for emergencies) 
  • Learn how to pay a credit card bill on time and IN FULL whenever possible 

Beware: 

  • If not used appropriately, you can build debt rapidly
  • Financing fees can add up quickly if you don’t pay your monthly bill in full 
  • You don’t want to ruin your credit score at a young age
