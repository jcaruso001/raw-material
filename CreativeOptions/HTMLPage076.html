Dual Enrollment Options in High School

Dual Enrollment programs allow eligible high school students to enroll in college coursework creditable toward both high school diploma requirements and some core college credits. This enables the student to shorten the time needed to complete college, broaden the scope of academic options available to them, and increase the depth of study.  Check with your high school guidance counselor for enrollment eligibility requirements such as your current high school GPA and qualifying test scores.
