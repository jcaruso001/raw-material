Paired Passages

The Reading Section will include a set of paired passages in one of the four passage types. Instead of one long passage there will be two passages addressing a particular subject. In this case work with one passage at a time. Read and answer the questions relating to the first passage. Then, read and answer the questions related to the second passage. Finally work on the questions (usually 3-4) that direct you to compare/contrast the two passages. You may be asked to compare the two authorsâ€™ thinking and/or point of view.
