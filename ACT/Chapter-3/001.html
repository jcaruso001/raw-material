The English Test

The English Test is composed of five passages presented in an essay format, each having 15 questions. Two types of questions are presented: those involving usage and mechanics and those involving rhetorical devices such as passage organization and author’s point of view. The English Test relies heavily on the conventions of standard English, which is the more formal English you find in material such as textbooks. These can be identified by underlining. Questions of a rhetorical nature involving text structure or the passage as a whole are identified by a number surrounded by a box. Many questions especially those involving grammar and usage have a “NO CHANGE” option as one of the answer choices. Do not be reluctant to pick this option.
