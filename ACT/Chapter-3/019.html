Author’s Purpose and Intent

Also on the English section, you may find questions such as: “If an editor wanted (something specific), would this essay meet his requirements?” Following the question will be two ‘yes’ answers and two ‘no’ answers. It is your job to pick either ‘yes’ or ‘no’ and then indicate which of the two has the correct reasoning.

You may also see a question or two of the following type: “If sentence (3) were deleted, what would be lost in the paragraph?” Then you would need to identify what the paragraph would lose if that particular sentence (or phrase) is deleted. The same is true for adding sentences or parts of sentences. You identify where in the paragraph the sentence would make the most sense if added.

Sequence

You may also find sequence question of the following type:

  1. Identify the correct sequence of sentence in a particular paragraph.
  2. Identify the correct sequence of paragraphs in a passage.
  3. Identify where a particular sentence should be placed in a paragraph.
  4. Identify where a particular paragraph should be placed in a passage.
