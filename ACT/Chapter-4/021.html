Solving Equations

This is one of the most important components of the exam. Algebraic expressions will appear in many different forms, ranging from simple linear equations to complex word problems. Some questions may ask you to solve for a specific value of x, while others may ask you to solve for x in terms of another variable or multiple variables.

To prepare you for the exam, we will first review solving basic linear equations, systems of linear equations and inequalities, and equations involving radical expressions.
