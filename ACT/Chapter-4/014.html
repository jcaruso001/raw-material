Just like with addition, when subtracting integers, you should always line everything up with the units column. Here are some of the things you need to remember with subtraction. Again, get more help if you feel that you need it.

  • When subtracting fractions, always find a common denominator. Consider the problem below.
  • When subtracting fractions written as mixed numbers, you don’t have to rewrite them as improper fractions. Subtract the integer components and then subtract the fractions.
