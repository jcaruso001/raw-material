Ratios and Proportions

Ratios are used to compare two quantities. They are typically written in one of two ways: as a fraction or with a colon (:). The ratio read as “4 to 10” could be written as 4/10 or 4:10. The ratio expressing the relationship between days to a week would be written as 7/1 or 7:1 because there are seven days in a week.

A proportion is a mathematical statement equating two ratios. Two ratios are said to be equal if, when written in fractional form, the fractions are equivalent fractions.
