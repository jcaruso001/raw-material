Solving Radical Expressions

The expression 2√x is a radical expression because it involves a root – in this case, the square
root of x.

The equation 2√x + 7 = 19 is radical because it involves a radical expression. You can solve this equation in the same way you solved other linear equations:

2√x + 7 = 19
2√x = 12
√x = 6
(√x)2 = 62
x = 36
