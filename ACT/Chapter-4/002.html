VIRTUAL TRANSLATORS

Virtual translators are computer programs that are capable of instant translation between human languages, even languages that use different character alphabets. They are available free of charge on various websites. Translating services that employ multilingual human beings are on the decline. As our world becomes increasingly interconnected, consumers have more need of translation services. That need is more and more likely to be filled by free automated web services. As a result, some have argued that there is less value in being multilingual. It is worth considering whether technology is changing our appreciate of learning other languages.

Perspective One: Digital technology and the Internet are changing our relationship with languages. Free translation is so plentiful and available that the value of learning a foreign language has been diminished significantly.

Perspective Two: Virtual translation may be helpful for translating simple business emails. However, the subtleties and connotation are often lost. There will always be a place for human beings in creating full and complete translations of important communications and works of art.

Perspective Three: The availability of virtual translators has opened up new possibilities and actually aids in the learning of new languages. Tools that were previously unavailable can be used to assist in learning and mastering new languages, making it easier than ever to become multilingual.

Essay Task

Writing a unified, coherent essay in which you evaluate multiple perspectives on the implications of virtual translators. In you essay, be sure to:

  • Analyze and evaluate the perspectives given
  • State and develop your own perspective on the issue
  • Explain the relationship; between your perspective and those given


Your perspective may be in full agreement with any of the others, in partial agreement, or wholly different. Whatever the case, support your ideas with logical reasoning and detailed, persuasive examples.
